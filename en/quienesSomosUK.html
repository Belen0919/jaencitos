JAENCITOS OILS

Crafting artisanal oils since 1825.

Hover over each image to learn more about us

Jaencitos, a venerable oil factory, has left an indelible mark since its foundation in 1825. Over the years, it has cultivated a solid reputation thanks to its unwavering commitment to the environment. Aware of the importance of sustainability, Jaencitos has implemented eco-friendly practices at every stage of its production process, from harvesting to packaging. The legacy of Jaencitos is not only based on the antiquity of its roots but also on the tireless hard work that has characterized the factory over the decades. The constant effort and dedication of its team have been crucial in maintaining the exceptional quality of its oils. Every drop of oil produced reflects the meticulousness and passion that have been the heart of Jaencitos since its early days. Jaencitos stands as a beacon in the industry, highlighting not only excellence in oil production but also a firm commitment to preserving the environment. Innovative agricultural methods and cutting-edge technologies come together to create products that not only satisfy the most discerning palates but also respect and protect our precious environment.

At the heart of the Jaencitos factory, the production of artisanal oils is an art that has been perfected with care and devotion since its humble beginnings in 1825. Every step of the process is executed with precision by skilled hands that have inherited tradition and generational knowledge. From the meticulous selection of the finest olives to the artisanal press, each phase reflects an unwavering commitment to authenticity and quality. In an exciting new stage, Jaencitos ventures beyond the boundaries of culinary consumption to introduce an innovative range of products. The factory, renowned for its excellence in olive oils, now embarks on creating oils intended not only for gastronomy but also for the overall well-being of the body and mind. This new collection seeks not only to delight the palate but also to offer relaxing and therapeutic properties. The fusion of natural ingredients and carefully selected essential oils results in products designed to nourish and revitalize the skin, as well as provide relaxing benefits during body massages.
